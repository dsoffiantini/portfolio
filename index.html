Shopper

Shopper was my personal project for DevMountain. In 2 weeks, I tried to create as many features of the Magento CMS as I could, as well as giving it a fashionable style. Some cool features: plenty of sliders, cross-sells, and an admin section that lets you manage your product catalog.

www.heartit.com

Heart It

Heart It was my no-server project at Devmountain, made after the first 4 weeks of class. Heart It is an image sharing gallery where users can click to like photos, share them with their friends, and also do some basic image editing with CSS and jQuery.
